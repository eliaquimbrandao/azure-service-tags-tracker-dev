What happens after I subscribe?

You'll receive a confirmation email with your subscription details and a unique unsubscribe link. Keep this email for future reference.

How often will I receive emails?

You'll receive weekly summaries every Monday, only when there are changes to Azure Service Tags.

Can I unsubscribe anytime?

Yes! Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt-out instantly with one click, and your confirmation email also contains a permanent unsubscribe link.

What if I try to subscribe with the same email twice?

The system will detect if your email is already subscribed and prevent duplicate subscriptions. Check your inbox for your original confirmation email.

Is my email address safe?

Absolutely. We never share your email with third parties. View our privacy policy for details.

What happens if I select specific services?

You'll only receive notifications when those specific services have IP range updates, reducing email noise.
